Home Decor Trends 2021 - A Comfortable Home

  • Next
  • Main
  • Stories
  • Interior Design
  • Home Decor Trends 2021 - A Comfortable Home

There’s a big focus on comfort (both in decor but also in color tones), a hunker for the familiar with modern ‘granny style’ and vintage decor and also sustainable design and a connection to nature will keep on becoming more and more important.

A Comfortable Home

Comfort is the most important "home decor trend" at the moment, it is probably what we’re all longing for. We’ve been spending more time at home than ever before, not just for relaxing but we’ve also started working at home.

Big comfy sofas in comfortable fabrics (velvet is still a big design trend) are something that we’ll see a lot in the future and this new seating is often in bright colors like pink, mustard yellow and green.

A Natural Look At Home

We've all been feeling a stronger connection to nature in the last year. Light-filled rooms with plants are gaining popularity.

Decorate your home with greenery, use window sills to grow herbs and add flower boxes to add a little to your home.

Also our furniture and home decor can add to that natural look with items made of natural materials such as wicker and rattan.

Natural elements such as terracotta vases, marble coffee tables, wicker baskets, and wooden furniture, all work together to add texture and warmth to a room, creating a more organic look that can produce a relaxed, calming vibe.

So we’re not only seeing green colors and natural materials in home decor but also more focus on sustainability.
