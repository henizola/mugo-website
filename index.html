ABOUT US

Welcome to Mugo Design+Build! We're a leading design and construction firm based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. Since its initiation in 2014 as a steel manufacturer, interior designer, and product supplier, we've grown into an independent design and construction powerhouse specializing in architecture and interior design. Our skilled team of architects, project managers, and finance administrators is dedicated to providing top-notch consulting, design, and production services to businesses and individuals. We don't just deliver designs; we forge ongoing learning partnerships by understanding your needs and challenges immediately.
