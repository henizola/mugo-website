Welcome to Mugo Design+Build! We're a leading design and construction ﬁrm based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. Since its initiation in 2014 as a steel manufacturer, interior designer, and product supplier, we've grown into an independent design and construction powerhouse specializing in architecture and interior design. Our skilled team of architects, project managers, and ﬁnance administrators is dedicated to providing top-notch consulting, design, and production services to businesses and individuals. We don't just deliver designs; we forge ongoing learning partnerships by understanding your needs and challenges immediately.

What sets us apart is our commitment to exceeding expectations. We collaborate with vendors who share our dedication to quality, ensuring every project is seamless and precise. Our portfolio includes impactful projects like the Arba-Minch Textile Factory, Mamokacha Hotel, and more.

Led by our CEO and Chief Creative Director, Selahadin Shamil, our experts bring a wealth of experience to every project, transforming spaces into functional, visually pleasing environments. As we look ahead, Mugo Design+Build aims to expand internationally, driven by our passion for design and excellence. Consider Mugo Design+Build for your next project, and experience the difference ﬁrsthand.

DESIGN &

BUILD

Our approach at Mugo Architects revolves around the "Design & Build" concept, emphasizing seamless integration of design and construction for successful projects. Our design team collaborates closely with the construction team from project inception, maintaining quality control, efficient communication, and on-time, on-budget delivery. We begin with a thorough needs analysis, prioritize transparent client communication, and develop innovative, functional designs. During construction, our skilled team ensures precision and sustainability, leveraging natural architecture and eco-friendly materials. Our goal is to create spaces that meet client needs while positively impacting the environment and communities. In summary, we offer collaboration, attention to detail, and a seamless Design and Build process, delivering exceptional quality and sustainable architectural projects that endure.
