GKD Metal Fabrics

GKD Metal Fabrics is a world-class leader in the technical metal weaving of stainless-steel architectural mesh systems. What began as a small group of groundbreaking German engineers in 1925 has evolved to become the most advanced technical metal weaving mill in the world.

Inox-Color

Inox-Color is one of the most efficient companies in the field of stainless steel and one of the first addresses for the versatile application of this material in architecture, building trade, industry, elevator construction and shop fitting.

Bemo

Bemo builds roofs which expand the horizon of the doable. They give planners and architects the freedom they need to break away from conventions. At the same time they satisfy people’s basic need for safe roofs and facades. Using state-of-the-art high-tech systems.
