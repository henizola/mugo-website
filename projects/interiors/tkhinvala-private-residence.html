Designed to cater to a new generation of homeowners seeking an international-standard premium lifestyle, the interior has the facilities that focus on health and pleasure. This unique facility is a place for relaxation, entertainment, physical activity, and spending leisure time with family and friends.

A soft and serene neutral color palette as well as a variety of stones and textures creates a deeply luxurious yet comfortable ambiance.

The living space of an individual house project is tailored to the needs of the modern family with reasonably distributed layout.

The unique color scheme, different natural materials like stone and wood create an elegant and sophisticated space, which is enriched with decorative elements.

One of the most eye-catching design element of this interior is the beautiful bar made of white Alabaster glass, which is illuminated from the inside, creating an unforgettable impression.
