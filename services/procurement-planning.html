Procurement Tracker

The procurement tracker is a specially made tool that enables project stakeholders to obtain accurate and online access to all procurement activities such as tender issuance, tender receipts, contract placement, shipment notifications, custom notices and on-site product delivery.

Careful procurement planning is an important factor for maintaining satisfactory results in compliance with the project delivery to its actual form and preventing delays arising from lack of materials or equipment delivered to the construction site.

Procurement Capabilities Include:

+  Procurement schedule
+  Tendering execution and analysis
+  Financial transactions and buy-out
+  Customs and logistics
+  Storage allocation
+  Project/Site delivery
