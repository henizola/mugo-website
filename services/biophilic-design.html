Biophilic Design

  • Main
  • Services
  • Biophilic Design

Biophilia is mankind’s innate biological connection with nature. Biophilic design can reduce stress, enhance creativity and clarity of thought, improve our well-being and expedite healing.

As the world population continues to urbanize, these qualities are ever more important.

Our design team is elevating an intuitive inclusion of Biophilic elements to the level of specific design choices with measurable benefits.

We strive to bring nature into buildings, reconnect people with natural elements and embrace the buildings with nature. 

Methodology

We start analyzing the project by thinking about its specific connections to nature and its spatial relationship to the site. This includes clients' inner desire and ability to see beyond our immediate surroundings.

The strongest space experiences are achieved through the creation of deliberate and engaging spatial configurations designed with patterns of Nature in the Space.

By Incorporating a diverse range of design strategies we can accommodate the needs of various user groups from various cultures and demographics to create an environment that is psycho-physiologically and cognitively restorative.

Biophilic Design capabilities include:

+  Natural Analogues
+  Space exploration of natural elements
+  Climate, ecology and the vernacular access
+  Daylight design and patterns
+  Biophilic Patterns
